Welcome

In 2012 Orbital Education established its second school in Moscow, the English International School, Moscow West. The new campus, for children 3 – 11 years of age, is located in the West of Moscow at Molodogvardeyskaya, near Kutuzovskiy Prospect.

Welcome

In 2006, Orbital Education established the English International School, Moscow East in Novogireevo, a pleasant and quiet suburb, 17 minutes from the centre of Moscow, by Metro. It quickly gained respect and popularity within the expatriate and Russian communities for the quality of education it provides to pupils aged 3 – 18. It is now a well-established market leader in the succesful provision of British style education.
